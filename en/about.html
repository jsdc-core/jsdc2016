About JSDC 2016

What's JSDC ?

JSDC (JavaScript Developer Conference) is the only annual JavaScript conference in Taiwan. One goal of this conference is share and bring the greatest and latest JavaScript developments and technologies to various levels of JavaScript Developers.

JavaScript Developers Conference

JSDC breaks the barrier, and integrates the power from independent developers, enterprises, and organizations.

We constantly put our endeavor in the globalization of knowledge, and help the attendees understand JavaScript in a different point of view.
