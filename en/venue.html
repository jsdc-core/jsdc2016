JSDC 2016 VEenue Location

Academia Sinica

Academia Sinica Address: No. 128, Sec. 2, Academia Rd., Nankang, Taipei 115, Taiwan － Humanities and Social Science Building(HSSB) International Conference Hall ( Google Map )

Transportation

By Car

Cars can directly go into AS campus with visitor registration at the main entrance. Visitor parking lot is located on B2 of the Humanities and Social Sciences Building (HSSB). Parking fee is NT$ 20 per hour.( Announcement )

By Metro (MRT)

There are two possible routes to the venue:

  1. Take Line 5 (Bannan Line) toNangang Exhibition Center Station (Exit 2), then take bus 205, 212, 276, 306, 620, 645, 679, S1, S5, or S12 to Academia Sinica Stop.
  2. Take Line 5 (Bannan Line) to Nangang Station (Exit 1), then take bus 212, 270 or Blue 25 to Academia Sinica Stop.

You could take a taxi instead of a bus with an average NT$ 80 fare. More info on MRT official site.

By City Bus

Take 205, 212, 212-Straight Line, 270, 270 (Shuttle), 276, 306, 306 (Shuttle), 620, 645, 645 (Extended), S1, S12, S12 (Shuttle), S5, BL25, and get off at Academia Sinica stop. City bus fare is either NT$ 15 or 30 (less than US$ 1), which depends on your distance and can be paid by either EasyCard or cash.
